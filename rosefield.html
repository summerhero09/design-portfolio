Rosefield Requiem

Description

  • Genre:
    Murder Mystery/Dating Sim
  • Made On:
    Ren’Py
  • Role:
    Project Lead, Story Director, UI Designer
  • Made with a 10-person team at@amoriemlabs, the Yale undergraduate game design club
  • 2023-2024
  • Demo Released! (Target Full Release: August 2024)

Valentina, a pop star from the small town of Rosefield, commits suicide on one winter night after returning secretly to the town. However, six months later, evidence emerges that it may not have been a suicide after all. The player must interact with four suspects and learn more about them…or get romantically involved with them. Decisions are key and the paths branch, each playthrough allowing for new evidence and facts to be uncovered.

Responsibilities

  • Write, edit, and lead the creation of the interactive narrative.
  • Organize assignments, timelines, and schedules for the team.
  • Design gameplay elements and lead creative direction.
  • Created cutscene art, UI elements, and icons.
  • Manage the Wiki and lore consistency
