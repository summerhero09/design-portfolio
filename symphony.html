Project Sympohony

Description

  • Role:
    Project Lead, Gameplay Designer, UI Designer
  • Made:
    2024 - Present
  • In development with a 20+ person team at@amoriemlabs, the Yale undergraduate game design club
State Machine

At an interdimensional music school, you, the player, must form the best band to defeat all others at the end of the semester at the battle of the bands. Bringing together two genres, the player can make story choices and recruit band members in a visual novel-like gameplay segment, and then unlock new songs and play them in a rhythm game to win high scores...and potentially make your band members like you more. The two distinct gameplay segments impact each other as the player learns more about the eclectic cast that goes from cat bassoon players to punk angel harpists.

  • Coordinate design concepts between teams and ensure goals and deadlines are met through Agile methodologies and Notion.
  • Design gameplay elements, game systems, and steer overall creative direction.
  • Design, create, and animate screens and UI elements.
  • Host weekly development meetings and guide iterative development.
